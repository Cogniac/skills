## I. What the application is doing?

The Area Detection application can detect the area covering the subject of interest (area mask) by asserting which pixels belong to the subject measuring the area of objects in images.

## II. Use-cases/ what problems the application can solve:

- Quality Control: Inspect products on the production line to detect defects and ensure quality.
- Smart city: Area detection apps are used in smart cities to monitor traffic flow, identify congestion, and detect illegal parking.

## III. What could cause issues while using the app:

- Sensitivity to image quality: The accuracy of area detection apps can be affected by the quality of the image being processed. If the image is blurry or noisy, the area detection app may have difficulty detecting areas accurately.
- Media is being captured from different angles.
- If the user has provided the wrong feedback.
- Computational requirements: Area detection apps can be computationally expensive to run, especially when detecting areas in large images or videos.
