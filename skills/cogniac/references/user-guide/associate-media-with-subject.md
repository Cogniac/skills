# Associate Media with Subject

We’ve added a new feature to the web app that makes it easier to keep track of important media items. Users can now ****tag media with any subject****, even if they don’t yet have a pipeline in place. This means that whenever you come across an image or video you know will be useful later, you no longer need to copy links into a spreadsheet or keep separate notes. Instead, you can save it directly in the platform by associating it with a subject of your choice.

## ****Why This Feature Was Built****

Previously, the only way to retain this content was by manually tracking it outside the system, typically in spreadsheets. This was time-consuming and also made it easy for things to be lost or forgotten.

By introducing the ability to associate media with a subject, we’ve streamlined that workflow. Now, all the media you want to save for future labeling or testing can live inside the platform itself, neatly organized and ready when you need it.

## ****How It Works****

- When viewing media, select “****Associate with Subject.****”
- Choose from your list of existing subjects.
- There are no restrictions—any subject can be used, regardless of pipeline ****input**** or ****output****.
- Confirm your choice, and the system will attach the media to the subject, displaying a green success message.
- Later, when your pipeline is ready, you can easily locate and use the media by subject.

## ****Benefits for Users****

- ****Stay organized:**** Keep all media in the platform—no more external spreadsheets.
- ****Flexible tagging:**** Assign media to any subject, independent of pipeline setup.
- ****Plan ahead:**** Collect and prepare media before pipelines are complete.
- ****Clear feedback:**** Each association is confirmed with a success message.
