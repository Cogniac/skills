# Usersnap Feature

## ****What is Usersnap:****

Usersnap enables users, testers, and stakeholders to easily capture screenshots of the Cogniac web app. They can enhance their feedback by adding annotations such as comments, drawings, and other visual cues. This valuable visual feedback is then effortlessly sent to project managers, developers, or support teams, providing a detailed report on issues, bugs, and user experiences.

## ****How Usersnap Works:****

Users can easily activate the Usersnap widget while browsing the Cogniac web app to capture screenshots or record their experience. They can enhance the images using powerful annotation tools, including freehand drawing, text comments, arrows, and highlights, ensuring that no detail is overlooked.
