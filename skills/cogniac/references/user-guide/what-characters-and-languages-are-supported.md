# What characters and languages are supported

****What characters and languages are supported?****

Cogniac documentation and products are written and formatted in English.

Throughout the system, all standard ascii characters are supported. Common alphanumeric characters are allowed in applications, subjects, tenants, and users names and descriptions. More specifically, the system supports at least the printable ASCII character set: <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ASCII#ASCII_printable_characters>

Specifically, Cogniac supports characters decimal 32 - 126 inclusive. If you have unique character requirements, please contact us at support@cogniac.ai
