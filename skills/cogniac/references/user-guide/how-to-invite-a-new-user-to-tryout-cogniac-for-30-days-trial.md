# How to invite a new User to tryout Cogniac for 30-days trial

## Objective:

Follow these steps to invite a new user to a 30-day free trial of Cogniac’s new Starter Plan.

## Prerequisite:

You should be an existing Cogniac User

## 1. Invite a new user to Cogniac by hovering over the Username and selecting the option “Invite Someone to tryout Cogniac”

![](../../../assets/media/images/how-to-invite-a-new-user-to-tryout-cogniac-for-30-days-trial-image-sx8gbmad.png)

## 2. Enter the Work Email address of the Person you wish to invite

![](../../../assets/media/images/how-to-invite-a-new-user-to-tryout-cogniac-for-30-days-trial-image-8bq7nnvl.png)

## 3. See the Confirmation that the Request has been sent for Cogniac Sales review

## ![](../../../assets/media/images/how-to-invite-a-new-user-to-tryout-cogniac-for-30-days-trial-image-6xb8y5yz.png)

## 4. Once the Cogniac Sales team approves your request, the person who has referred a New User gets a confirmation email indicating that their referral request has been approved

![](../../../assets/media/images/how-to-invite-a-new-user-to-tryout-cogniac-for-30-days-trial-image-e2zq8i1u.png)

## 5. New User also gets an email inviting them to a 30-day Free Trial Signup

![](../../../assets/media/images/how-to-invite-a-new-user-to-tryout-cogniac-for-30-days-trial-image-nki0016j.png)

## 6. When a new user selects the ‘Accept Invitation’ link, a 30-day free trial sign-up form opens, and the email auto-populates.

![](../../../assets/media/images/how-to-invite-a-new-user-to-tryout-cogniac-for-30-days-trial-image-7tifz3h3.png) ![](../../../assets/media/images/how-to-invite-a-new-user-to-tryout-cogniac-for-30-days-trial-image-s07nbqum.png)

## 7. After successful signup confirmation, the New Customer will be able to see the Webapp Login screen

![](../../../assets/media/images/how-to-invite-a-new-user-to-tryout-cogniac-for-30-days-trial-image-mof9b5rn.png)

## 8. Upon entering valid login credentials, new users should be able to successfully sign in to their Tenant and view a 'Welcome Message'.

![](../../../assets/media/images/how-to-invite-a-new-user-to-tryout-cogniac-for-30-days-trial-image-gcrh8n70.png)

## 9. Once the New User exits the Welcome screen, they should see the CloudCore homepage mentioning ‘Starter Plan’.

## ![](../../../assets/media/images/how-to-invite-a-new-user-to-tryout-cogniac-for-30-days-trial-image-ihdrd6tq.png)

## 10. New Users can also check the used and available compute resources under their plan and what their tenant consumes. They can find this information under the ‘Subscription Dashboard’ screen by selecting the Starter Plan from the homepage.

- The ‘Subscription Dashboard’ shows the ‘Starter Plan’ remaining free days, User Feedback per billing cycle, and #Number of model-based Active Apps & media Storage currently being Consumed. Please contact a Cogniac Sales representative or email sales@cogniac.ai for your Plan Information Details.

![](../../../assets/media/images/how-to-invite-a-new-user-to-tryout-cogniac-for-30-days-trial-image-cpa8z1jf.png)

![](../../../assets/media/images/how-to-invite-a-new-user-to-tryout-cogniac-for-30-days-trial-image-318cs1e6.png)

## 11. If a New User selects the ‘Upgrade’ button, they will get the following message

![](../../../assets/media/images/how-to-invite-a-new-user-to-tryout-cogniac-for-30-days-trial-image-apasl656.png)
